Top distribution customersiHow Zimed reaches patients. Most of our product does not go straight to pharmacies. National wholesalers (McKesson, Kohl & Frisch) are middlemen that re-sell and deliver Zimed to thousands of independent and banner pharmacies we never see on the invoice. Retail chains (Shoppers, Jean Coutu, Familiprix) buy centrally and stock their own stores, so one listing decision reaches hundreds of locations. Regional wholesalers (LPG, Imperial, Unipharm, Nu-Quest) cover provinces the big two serve thinly, for example Nu-Quest is the main route into Newfoundland. Demand is created upstream by the eye doctors who prescribe and at the pharmacy counter, so with wholesalers the goal is staying listed and in stock, while chains and prescribers are where direct selling moves the needle.
